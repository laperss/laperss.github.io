    About

    This is the personal webpage of Linnea Persson. I am a PhD Student at the department of Automatic Control since May 2016, within the Wallenberg Autonomous Systems Program, WASP under the supervision of Professor Bo Wahlberg. I graduated from the degree Programme in Engineering Physics in 2016, with a M.Sc in Systems, Control & Robotics.

    My interests include collabotarive control, optimization based control, autonomous systems and aerospace applications.
